How does the game

play work?

It is very simple. Stake $Rage tokens in the farm and earn points. You then use the points to redeem NFTs. Stake these NFTs in the many tournaments available on the platform to win rewards. Each NFT has its own utility value and you will earn points based on it.

Let us take the Rafael Nadal NFT for example. You can stake it to play the French Open or Wimbledon. You will earn on every match he wins. Put your game knowledge to test as you might want to use a Roger Federer NFT for Wimbledon. And keep the Nadal NFT for French Open.
